Hello, World! My name is George, and I'm a software engineer at FreeAgent. My job is to help build and maintain an award-winning accounting platform used by over 100,000 businesses. I'm also passionate about mental health, communication, and facilitating a healthy team culture.

Prior to joining FreeAgent, I studied computer science at the University of Birmingham. During my degree, I completed a research project on conformity in online behaviour, inspired by Solomon Asch's famous conformity experiments.

I've worked with many languages and frameworks such as Django, React, Swift, and in my current role, Ruby on Rails. I believe that simplicity is the ultimate sophistication; software should be carefully written when developed, and simple when used.

In my spare time, I enjoy photography, playing tennis, and listening to music.
